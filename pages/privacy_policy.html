Introduction

Welcome to Easy Python Guide. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website and use our services.

Information We Collect

Contact Form Information

When you use our contact form, we collect:

  • Your name

  • Your message content

This information is processed through FormSubmit, a third-party form handling service. Please review FormSubmit's privacy policy for information about how they handle your data.

Analytics Information

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This service collects:

  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • General location information (country/city level)

This information is collected anonymously and helps us improve our educational content and user experience. Please review Google's privacy policy for information about how they handle analytics data.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except:

  • FormSubmit processes contact form submissions on our behalf
  • Google Analytics processes usage data according to their privacy policy
  • When required by law or to protect our rights

Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies through Google Analytics to:

  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Remember your preferences
  • Provide anonymized insights into how our content is used

You can control cookies through your browser settings and opt out of Google Analytics tracking by visiting Google's opt-out page or using browser extensions that block analytics tracking.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Request data portability

International Users

Easy Python Guide is operated from the United Kingdom. If you are accessing our website from outside the UK, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the UK.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
